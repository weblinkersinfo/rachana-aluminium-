# Blueprint Summary

**Information Grouping Principles Used:**
- **Z-Pattern (Desktop):** Users scan left to right, top to bottom. Imagery is placed on the left or full-width, text on the right or centered.
- **F-Pattern (Mobile):** Users scroll down quickly. Icons and bold headers catch the eye.
- **Cards & Grids:** Used extensively to avoid long paragraphs and make information "snackable".
- **Visual Weight:** Highest visual weight is always given to Trust Signals (In-house manufacturing, project photos, experience numbers).
