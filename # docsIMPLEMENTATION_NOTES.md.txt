# Rachana Aluminium - Implementation Notes

## Project Goal

Build a website that represents the real personality and philosophy of Rachana Aluminium.

The website should communicate:

* Trust
* Quality
* Professionalism
* Craftsmanship
* Relationships
* Personal Attention

---

## Design Direction

Style:

Minimal Luxury + Modern Architecture

Inspired by:

* Apple's cleanliness
* Architecture studio sophistication
* Warm family-business relationships

Avoid:

* Flashy animations
* Overly luxurious aesthetics
* Corporate appearance
* Aggressive sales language
* Excessive call-to-actions
* Information overload

---

## Design Personality

The website should feel:

* Elegant
* Reassuring
* Human
* Professional
* Modern
* Calm
* Architecture-inspired

---

## Color Direction

Primary Colors:

* White
* Charcoal Grey
* Black

Accent Color:

* Red (from company logo)

Customer-preferred product colors:

* Grey (most popular)
* Black
* White

---

## Content Philosophy

Show first.
Explain second.

Use:

* Images
* Cards
* Icons
* Short paragraphs
* Clear hierarchy
* White space

Avoid:

* Walls of text
* Technical overload
* Long paragraphs

---

## Product Philosophy

Rachana Aluminium does not simply sell windows and doors.

The company provides:

Customization + Guidance + Manufacturing + Installation + After-Sales Service

Every product page should help customers make informed decisions.

---

## Selection Guide

Every product should include a Selection Guide.

Purpose:
Help customers understand which solution suits their requirements.

The guide should educate without overwhelming.

---

## Customization Options

Products can be customized using:

* Colours
* Glass types
* Handles
* Locks
* Mesh options

Recommendations are also provided by the company.

---

## Manufacturing

Approximately 90% of manufacturing work is handled internally.

Internal processes include:

* Precision cutting
* Fabrication
* Assembly
* Packaging
* Quality checks

Powder coating and certain on-site installation activities are handled externally.

This should be highlighted as a trust factor.

---

## Customer Segments

Primary Customers:

* Builders
* Architects
* Engineers
* Contractors

Secondary Customers:

* Homeowners
* Residential customers
* Middle-class homeowners
* Premium homeowners

---

## Website Goal

When a visitor leaves the website, they should think:

"This company looks professional."

"These people genuinely care about quality."

"I can trust them with my project."

"This company feels human."
