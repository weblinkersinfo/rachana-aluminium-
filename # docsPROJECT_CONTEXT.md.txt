# Rachana Aluminium - Project Context

## Company Overview

Rachana Aluminium is an aluminium windows and doors company established in 2012. The owner has more than 25 years of industry experience in the aluminium industry.

The company operates across multiple states and has successfully completed more than 500 projects.

### Service Regions

* Maharashtra
* Goa
* Gujarat
* North Karnataka
* Rajasthan (selected projects)

### Major Cities Served

* Kolhapur
* Ichalkaranji
* Pune
* Mumbai
* Goa
* North Karnataka
