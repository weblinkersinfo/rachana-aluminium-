## 2 — Brand Philosophy & Values

The brand sits at the intersection of **craftsmanship** and **human relationships**. This is *not* a faceless manufacturer — it is a family-business culture led by a calm, humble, people-first owner who views workers as the company's backbone.

**Core values (in priority order):**

1. **Trust** — earned through transparency and consistency
2. **Quality** — visible in materials, processes, and finished work
3. **Personal Touch** — every client gets individual attention
4. **People First** — workers, clients, and partners are treated as family

**Owner's personality traits that the website must mirror:**
Calm · Composed · Humble · Friendly · Professional · Relationship-driven · Quietly confident

## 3 — Website Purpose & Strategic Intent

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This is **not** an aggressive lead-generation or e-commerce site.
> It is a **premium brand showcase and information website**.

The site must:
- Establish a professional digital presence
- Showcase products and completed projects
- Build trust and credibility at first glance
- Reflect the company's philosophy authentically
- Help customers make *informed* decisions (not push sales)

**Desired visitor takeaway:**
*"This company looks professional. They genuinely care about quality. They feel human. I can trust them with my project."*

## 8 — Context Corrections (Source of Truth)

> [!CAUTION]
> This section contains authoritative corrections from the client. These override all previous assumptions and must not be modified unless explicitly instructed.

### Product Series — NOT Tiers

The three System Window series (25mm, 29mm, 32mm) represent **profile dimensions**, not quality levels. Do NOT use language like Basic / Premium / Flagship / Budget / Entry-level.

All three series are: Durable · Elegant · Smooth · Premium-looking

Series selection depends on:
- Opening size & window dimensions
- Structural requirements
- Customer preferences & project requirements
- The 32mm series is generally used for larger openings (~10 ft +) due to span and weight handling

### Website Positioning

The website **is**: Professional digital presence · Premium brand showcase · Information website · Trust-building platform

The website is **NOT**: E-commerce · Quotation portal · High-conversion funnel · Aggressive lead-gen

### Communication Philosophy

Core message: *"We are experienced professionals who genuinely care about quality and relationships."*

The site should feel: Elegant · Warm · Trustworthy · Human · Professional · Architecture-inspired

Never feel: Corporate · Sales-heavy · Pushy · Over-designed

### Brand Voice

Calm and confident. Short sentences. Honest, human language.

Avoid: Exaggerated marketing copy · Buzzwords · Corporate jargon · Aggressive sales messaging

### Guiding Principle

**Show first. Explain second.**
Visual storytelling always takes priority over long text sections.

## 10 — My Observations & Strategic Notes

> [!TIP]
> **Dual audience tension.** The primary audience (builders, architects) values specs, process, and credibility markers. The secondary audience (homeowners) values visual beauty, simple language, and guidance. The Selection Guide per product is a smart bridge — it should be designed as a lightweight, visual decision-aid rather than a data table.

> [!NOTE]
> **In-house manufacturing (~90 %)** is the single most differentiating trust signal. It deserves prominent, recurring treatment — ideally a short video loop or photo sequence of the workshop, not just a text bullet.

> [!NOTE]
> **The owner's personality IS the brand.** The "About Us" and overall tone must embody calm confidence and humility — not boastful copy. First-person plural ("We believe…") with short, honest sentences will carry this voice better than third-person marketing language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **"Show first. Explain second"** is the guiding content principle. This means hero sections should be image-dominant (real project photography, not stock), and text sections should be scan-friendly cards or icon grids — never long prose blocks.
